Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Plotra?
Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, verify your email, and start mapping your farm parcels. Our onboarding guide will walk you through each step.
What is EUDR compliance and why does it matter?
EUDR (European Union Deforestation Regulation) requires companies to ensure their products don't contribute to deforestation. For coffee farmers, this means providing traceability from farm to consumer. Plotra helps you generate the required due diligence statements automatically.
How accurate is the farm mapping feature?
Our mapping uses GPS technology with accuracy within 2-5 meters. You can also use our walking feature for even more precise boundary mapping in the field.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your farm information is only accessible to authorized users within your cooperative or organization.
