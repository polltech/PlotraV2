Last Updated: April 4, 2026

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Plotra ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our coffee farming management platform.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Farm location and operational data
  • Account credentials and preferences
  • Communication records with our support team

Farm and Agricultural Data

As part of our service, we collect agricultural data including:

  • Farm boundaries and GPS coordinates
  • Crop yields and production records
  • Soil and environmental data
  • Compliance and certification information

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our platform:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP addresses and location data
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Performance and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain our coffee farming platform
  • Compliance: To ensure adherence to agricultural regulations (EUDR, etc.)
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • Communication: To send important updates and respond to inquiries
  • Legal Compliance: To meet legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Cooperatives: With your cooperative for collective management purposes
  • Regulators: When required by law or for compliance verification
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help operate our platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by court order or legal process

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Secure data centers and infrastructure
  • Regular backup and disaster recovery procedures

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 3 years after deactivation
  • Farm Data: Retained indefinitely for compliance and historical records
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 2 years for security and analytics purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing in certain circumstances

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. For detailed information, please see our Cookie Policy.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Kenya. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by email or through our platform.
